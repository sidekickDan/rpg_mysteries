# Notes for Creator

## What to Avoid in Public Posts

Do not reveal:

- Culprit
- Motive
- Method
- Timeline trick
- Key contradiction
- Final clue
- True alibi
- Secret relationship
- Hidden evidence

## What Is Safe to Mention

You may mention:

- Case title
- Time period
- Setting
- Victim name, if not a spoiler
- Number of suspects
- General tone
- General mystery type
- Difficulty options
- Clean content rating
- Copy/paste gameplay format

## Best Posting Pattern

1. Post the teaser.
2. Post the full copy/paste prompt.
3. Ask people to comment their suspect.
4. Later, post the reveal.
5. Ask whether they solved it.
6. Tease the next case.
