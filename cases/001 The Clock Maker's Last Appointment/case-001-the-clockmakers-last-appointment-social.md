**The Clockmaker’s Last Appointment** is a clean, fair-play mystery RPG prompt designed for ChatGPT.

You play as the investigator in a late-1800s English village where a respected clockmaker has been found dead in his locked workshop. The clock has stopped. The door was bolted from inside. Five suspects have reasons to hide the truth.

The culprit, motive, method, timeline, clues, and solution are locked before the game begins. The AI host should not change the answer to match your guesses.
