- Who did you accuse?
- Which clue changed your mind?
- Did the clock timeline fool you?

1. Restate the case premise.
2. Warn that the solution is coming.

4. Reveal the culprit only after a clear spoiler countdown.
5. Explain the clue chain briefly.
6. Invite players to try the next case.
