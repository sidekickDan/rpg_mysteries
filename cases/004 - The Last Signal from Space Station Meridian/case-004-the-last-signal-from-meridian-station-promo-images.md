# Case #004 Social Promo Image Prompts

These prompts create two independent, spoiler-free promotional images. Do not combine them into a collage, diptych, split panel, or multi-layout design. They are social advertisements, not in-game reference images.

## Image 1 — Square 1:1

Create exactly one polished square 1:1 promotional poster for a clean interactive science-fiction murder mystery. Show the interior of an isolated late-22nd-century research station: a curved observation window overlooking the enormous dark-blue edge of a rogue planet, cool white and deep navy lighting, restrained amber emergency lights, and a central life-support display reading “07:12:00.” Suggest five human suspects only through five distant, distinct silhouettes reflected in the observation glass. No visible body, weapon, blood, violence, aliens, monsters, horror imagery, or culprit clues. The mood is tense, intelligent, cinematic, mysterious, and PG—not frightening.

Make the typography exceptionally legible on a phone. Use a premium cinematic science-fiction title treatment with strong hierarchy. Include exactly this promotional copy, spelled correctly:

Top small brand line: “HIDDEN TRAIL MYSTERIES”

Large title:
“CASE #004”
“THE LAST SIGNAL FROM MERIDIAN STATION”

Short hook:
“Five suspects. Seven hours of air. Everyone is lying.”

Compact how-to-play line near the bottom:
“Download • Add to your AI • Say ‘Let’s begin.’”

Small footer:
“Hidden Trail Games”
“Every story leaves a trail.”
“hiddentrailmysterygames@gmail.com”

Keep generous margins and reserve clean negative space behind all text. Do not add extra names, fake reviews, logos, interface buttons, paragraphs, suspect labels, solution hints, or additional text. Exactly one square image.

## Image 2 — Vertical Portrait 4:5

Create exactly one polished vertical portrait 4:5 promotional poster for a clean interactive psychological science-fiction murder mystery. Compose the image for mobile social media. The upper portion looks through Meridian Station’s tall observation window at the dim crescent edge of a rogue planet with sparse distant stars. Inside the station, use sleek but believable late-22nd-century architecture, cool steel-blue light, narrow amber emergency strips, and a life-support display reading “07:12:00.” In the lower middle distance, show five small human crew silhouettes separated by the station’s architecture, implying distrust and conflicting stories. Do not show a victim, weapon, blood, violence, aliens, monsters, horror imagery, or any clue that identifies the culprit. The atmosphere is isolated, cerebral, urgent, clean, and PG.

## Shared Visual Continuity Notes

- Use the same station design, planet color, navy/amber palette, and title typography in both independently generated images.
- Keep five silhouettes visually distinct but anonymous and nonthreatening.
- The countdown is atmosphere only and must not expose how the life-support failure occurred.
- Proofread all displayed text. If the image model cannot reliably render the full footer, prioritize series name, case number, case title, and central hook, then add smaller copy during graphic layout.
