## Spoiler-Free Reveal Post Template

**Spoiler warning for Case #002: The Midnight Signal at Waverly Station.**

Before revealing the answer, ask viewers:

- Who did you accuse?
- What clue changed your mind?
- Did you trust what everyone heard?

Reveal structure without spoilers in the caption:

1. Remind players of the central question.
2. Explain that the solution depends on the timeline.
3. Mention that one “live” detail should have been questioned.
4. Invite viewers to play before watching the full reveal.

Do not reveal the culprit, motive, method, true timeline trick, final clue, secret evidence, or hidden relationships in spoiler-free posts.
